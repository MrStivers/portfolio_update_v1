Background

Ryan Stivers is an educational consultant who also serves as the Founding Developer Coach at the Rooted School in New Orleans, Louisiana. As an educational researcher and practitioner, he has leveraged his expertise and experience to support numerous school systems and nonprofits through significant change management efforts. Stivers utilizes quantitative and qualitative data and analysis to develop technology driven academic and non-academic systems – in particular those related to blended, personalized, and distance learning models. Additionally, he holds deep expertise in developing equity-based, culturally competent, and social justice themed curricula for students across the K-12 spectrum, non-traditional adult learners, and those in alternative educational settings.

Stivers is a passionate social justice and educational equity advocate within the community. He is a New Leaders Council – Louisiana Chapter Fellow and an active member of Kappa Delta Pi International Honor Society in Education where he was previously elected to the international board. Stivers is also an inductee and member of Phi Beta Kappa Honor Society and Omicron Delta Kappa National Leadership Honor Society. He serves on numerous nonprofit boards and volunteers his time to activities that promote youth and community development. Stivers holds a B.A. Summa Cum Laude in Secondary Education and History from Lake Forest College, where he was a Christopher Mojekwu Awardee for Intercultural Relations and President’s Awardee, as well as a M.A. in Educational Leadership from Teachers College at Columbia University.
